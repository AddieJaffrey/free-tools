About This Tool

This free online PDF to Word converter lets you quickly turn your PDF documents into editable Word files. It’s completely free, works in your browser, and requires no registration or installation.

How to Use

  • Click "Choose File" and upload your PDF.
  • Click "Convert to Word" to start the process.
  • Your Word document will download automatically once conversion is done.

Limitations

This tool works best with text-based PDFs. PDFs containing only images, scanned pages, or complex tables may not convert perfectly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my file safe? Yes, all processing happens in your browser. No files are uploaded anywhere.

Does it work on mobile? Yes, it works on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

Will it preserve images or tables? Basic text is preserved; complex tables and images may not convert perfectly.
